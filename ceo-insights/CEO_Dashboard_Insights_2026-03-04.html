CEO Watch List - Areas Requiring Attention

1. TX160 Churn at 15.31% - Highest in Network

Despite strong subscriber growth (+58/mo), TX160's elevated churn means it's working harder to stay ahead. If churn were brought to TX190's level (12.26%), TX160 would retain an additional ~32 members per month. The improving trend from 16.74% (Dec) to 13.03% (late Feb) reversed sharply in early March. Investigate root cause.

2. UT426 Single Wash Price of $6.65 - Far Below Network

TX190 achieves $17.01 and TX160 achieves $12.82 per single wash. UT426's $6.65 is 61% below TX190. Even a $2 increase would add ~$2,700/month in single wash revenue (based on ~45 singles/day). Evaluate market pricing and promotional strategies.

3. TX190 Trial Conversion at 11.8% - Lowest in Network

TX160 converts trials at 22.4% and UT426 at 17.2%, but TX190 only converts 11.8%. With 320 trial members, improving conversion by even 5 percentage points would add ~16 paying members per month. Review the trial-to-paid experience at TX190.

4. TX190 Capture Rate Declining

TX190's capture rate dropped from ~24% range to 21.8%, down 2.2pp vs the prior 30-day period. With 52 opportunities per day, each percentage point of capture rate represents roughly 0.5 additional members captured daily (~15/month). Investigate whether this is a staffing issue, process change, or seasonal pattern.

5. TX160 Highest Volume Site - 80 Opps/Day

TX160 generates the most daily opportunities (80 vs TX190's 52 and UT426's 58). Combined with its 22.4% trial conversion rate, TX160 is the growth engine of the network. Continue investing in this site's growth trajectory.
